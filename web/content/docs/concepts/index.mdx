Understanding these concepts will help you get the most out of m1n3.

## Mining Shares

Mining shares are proof-of-work submissions that are verified and recorded on the Sui blockchain. In m1n3, they are tradeable on-chain assets.

[Learn more about shares →](/docs/concepts/shares)

## Block Templates

Block templates define which Bitcoin transactions miners work on. Node runners create templates, and miners choose which template to direct their hashpower to.

[Learn more about templates →](/docs/concepts/templates)

## Hashprice

Hashprice measures how much revenue a unit of mining power generates per day. It's the key metric for valuing mining shares.

[Learn more about hashprice →](/docs/concepts/hashprice)

## Difficulty

Bitcoin's difficulty adjustment mechanism ensures blocks are found approximately every 10 minutes, regardless of how much hashrate is on the network.

[Learn more about difficulty →](/docs/concepts/difficulty)

## Staking

Stake M1N3 tokens on block templates to signal confidence and earn a share of the 2% marketplace trading fee.

[Learn more about staking →](/docs/concepts/staking)
